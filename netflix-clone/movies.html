BhootPolice

"Bhoot Police" is a horror-comedy film directed by Pavan Kirpalani, following two ghost hunters who find themselves tackling supernatural forces in a haunted forest. The movie offers a blend of scares and laughs as the duo confronts paranormal entities while navigating their own strained relationship.
